To append the loupe and the optional lightbox, simply click on the image. The image is the target. Clicking inside the loupe will detach it and the optional lightbox.

The loupe will stop just outside the edges of the smaller image. If your cursor leaves it, the loupe will pulse indicating that your cursor has left the area. If your cursor goes back to the loupe, it'll pick up where it last left off.


If there is a lightbox, clicking outside the loupe will automatically detach both the loupe and lightbox.



Or, if there is no lightbox, clicking anything outside the loupe will automatically detach it.



Loading images with AJAX

Or, if there's an error loading the image, a message will be displayed.



When loading an image, a loading div will append to the loupe and detach after an ajax success or error.
